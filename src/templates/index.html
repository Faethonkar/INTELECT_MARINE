About INTELECT MARINE

INTELECT MARINE is a specialized technical workshop in Marine and Industrial Automation, carrying over 30 years of experience in the industry. Founded by Charalampos Chatzikonstantinou, an electronics engineer who has been working in the industry since the 1990s.

Our team expanded in 2018 with the addition of Eleftherios Tsirtsiridis, bringing additional expertise to our operations. We hold BUREAU VERITAS ISO 9001 Certification, ensuring the highest standards of quality in all our services.

The main bulk of our operations is in the Maritime industry, serving bulk carriers, containers, tankers, reefers, ferry boats, cruisers, motor and sailing yachts, and navy ships.
