About INTELECT MARINE

Three decades of excellence in marine and industrial automation

Our Foundation

INTELECT MARINE is a specialized technical workshop in Marine and Industrial Automation, carrying over 30 years of experience in the industry. Founded by Charalampos Chatzikonstantinou, an electronics engineer who has been working in the industry since the 1990s.

Our journey began with a vision to provide cutting-edge automation solutions for the maritime industry, combining technical expertise with innovative approaches to solve complex challenges.

Growth & Expansion

Our team expanded in 2018 with the addition of Eleftherios Tsirtsiridis, bringing additional expertise to our operations. This expansion allowed us to take on more complex projects and serve a broader range of clients.

We hold BUREAU VERITAS ISO 9001 Certification, ensuring the highest standards of quality in all our services. This certification reflects our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement.
